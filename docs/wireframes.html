Delivery-state language (used everywhere): queued (no link yet) → ⛓ sent to mesh (handed to ≥1 peer, may be carried physically) → ✓✓ delivered (recipient's device confirmed via gossiped receipt). The transport chip in the app bar shows the live path: LAN (same Wi-Fi), Wi-Fi Direct (hotspot/group), Internet (direct P2P), or Carrying (offline, storing for later hops). The UI never claims delivery it can't prove — that honesty is a core design rule from ARCHITECTURE.md.
